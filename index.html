GLOSARIO

Trap
A mixture of rap, hip hop, and dubstep, trap is a musical subgenre of hip hop, originating in the 1990s in the southern United States.
Cumbias
Popular dance originating in Panama, Colombia and other Latin American countries, with slow movement and a tropical rhythm, performed by couples who turn without touching.
Rock
Rock is a broad genre of popular music originating as rock and roll in the early 1950s in the United States.
Reggae
The word reggae (folk music from Jamaica that combines calypso with blues), comes from Jamaican English, from a song by The Maytals from 1968 called "Do the Reggay"
Corridos
The concept of corrido refers to a literary and musical form from Mexico that emerged at the end of the 18th century. They are sung pieces, generally accompanied by an instrument, which are characterized by narrating popular events and deeds of real or fictional heroes and leaders.
